Software Development Life Cycle Methodologies

Introduction to SDLC | Waterfall Model | V Shaped Model | Spiral Model

A V-shaped model is an extension of the waterfall model, the process steps are bent upwards after the
implementation and coding phase. The major difference between the V-shaped model and waterfall model
is the early test planning. In the V-shaped model software requirements are clearly defined and known.
