Software Development Life Cycle Methodologies

Introduction to SDLC | Waterfall Model | V Shaped Model | Spiral Model

The spiral model is favored for large, expensive, and complicated projects. This model uses many of the
same phases as the waterfall model, in essentially the same order, separated by planning, risk assessment,
and the building of prototypes and simulations. It is used in the large applications and systems which
built-in small phases or segments.
