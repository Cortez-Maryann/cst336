How To Play

Based on the classic game of rock paper scissors, I bring to you an Overwatch Alternative!
You have three characters with different abilities that allow you to defeat one.

Reinhardt is a close combat fighter who uses a shield for protection.
Weakness: projectiles
Strength: shield

Junkrat shoots projectiles that can destroy sheilds quickly.
Weakness: snipers
Strength: projectiles

Widowmaker is a sniper that can shoot from a safe distance, but cannot shoot through sheilds.
Weakness: shields
Strength: sniper
